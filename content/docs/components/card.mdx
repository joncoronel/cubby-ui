## API Reference

The Card is a custom component that does not extend Base UI.

### Props

#### Card

Container component for grouping related content with optional header, body, and footer sections.

<ApiPropsList>

<ApiProp
  name="variant"
  fullType='"default" | "inset"'
  simpleType="string"
  defaultValue='"default"'
>
  Visual style variant of the card.

  - `"default"` — a single elevated surface (the standard card).
  - `"inset"` — a gray-framed outer with a lifted inner card. Reads as "a Default Card wrapped in a tray."
</ApiProp>

<ApiProp name="level" fullType="1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8" defaultValue="3">
  Surface elevation level for the card. See the [Surfaces](/docs/getting-started/surfaces) docs. For the `inset` variant, only the outer frame's shadow/rim tracks this prop — the gray-frame bg and the inner card's level are pinned for the visual idiom to hold.
</ApiProp>

<ApiProp name="shadowLevel" fullType="1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8" defaultValue="1">
  Shadow weight. Defaults to `1` — a flat outlined card (rim only, no drop shadow). Matches the rest of the flat-card family (Table, DataTable, code-block, ComponentPreview).
</ApiProp>

</ApiPropsList>
